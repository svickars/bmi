## Description

The Big Mac Index (BMI) is a crowdsourced take on The Economist’s famous index — built by people who actually eat the burgers.

Log what you paid, rate the quality, add photos, and tag where you ate: airports, highway stops, downtown, and more. BMI normalizes every report with live exchange rates and US inflation data so a meal from last year still compares fairly today.

Features:
• Sign in with Apple and a unique public username
• Global feed, map, and statistics
• Friend linking, tags, reactions, and activity notifications
• CloudKit sync for public reports and photos
• Currency comparison in your locale or a currency you choose

Whether you’re traveling or curious about local prices, BMI turns Big Mac runs into real global price intelligence.
