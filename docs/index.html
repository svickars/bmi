The Big Mac Index

Crowdsource McDonald's Big Mac prices, ratings, and photos. Compare meals around the world with live exchange rates and inflation-adjusted "today's dollars."

Join TestFlight View on GitHub

Replace the TestFlight link in docs/index.html once your build is uploaded.

📍 Report meals

Log price, rating, location type, GPS, photos, and who you were with. Tag friends who shared the run.

🌍 Global index

Public reports sync through CloudKit. See feed, map, and stats normalized to your comparison currency.

👥 Social

Link friends by username, get activity notifications, and react to reports with emoji.

💱 Fair comparisons

Frankfurter live FX plus US CPI inflation adjustment so last year's Tokyo run still compares to today's SF run.
